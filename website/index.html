About JG Foods

A proper butcher.
On your doorstep.

JG Foods is a mobile butcher run by Jon Green, delivering quality fresh meat to homes and businesses across North Liverpool, Ormskirk and West Lancashire. What started as a passion for proper, no-nonsense butchery has grown into a service that hundreds of local families and food businesses rely on every week.

Jon delivers in a fully refrigerated van through the week — which means you're getting the same quality you'd expect from a high street butcher, without having to leave the house. Every cut is sourced carefully, and you'll always deal with Jon directly. No call centres, no middlemen.

With a 5-star food hygiene rating, 100% recommendation score and hundreds of loyal customers across the region, JG Foods has built its reputation the old-fashioned way — by turning up on time, every time, with meat worth eating.
